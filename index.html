How to make

step.1 collectt

We collect plastic wastes generated in the manufacturing process of daily products as well as plastics thrown away in daily life and strives to reduce carbon emissions by minimizing the process of manufacturing recycled plastics.

step.2 crushh

Collected plastics are crushed into a small particles, the suitable size for reuse.

step.3 sortt

The crushed plastic flakes are sorted as intended use, type, and color after inspection and cleaning.

step.4 meltt

Sorted plastic flakes are melted to become flexible to be created in new shape.

step.5 craftt

Through compression and processing, new shape of plastic is constructed.
Recycling plastic saves 76% energy compared to creating a new products with new materials.

step.6 rebirth

Plastic is not a one-off, cheap, or worthless thing.
Plastic Ark provides a new life to plastics to become meaningful and valuable use, believing that they are no longer a threat to the earth, living things, and humanity.
